# Bunny Siberian Model

## Identity

Bunny Siberian is the **systems company** of the GrupsBunny holding.

## Business Model

- **Type**: Empresa de sistemas (B2B)
- **Revenue Model**: Receita Recorrente (SaaS + Services)
- **Products**:
  - Sistema Siberian ERP (sale, implementation, support)
  - Consulting services
  - System implementation
  - Technical support

## Revenue Streams

1. **MRR** — Monthly Recurring Revenue from SaaS/ERP clients
2. **Implementation Fees** — One-time fees for system deployment
3. **Support Contracts** — Ongoing maintenance and support
